## Business

What does the company sell?

NBIS provides infrastructure for AI training and inference workloads.

The historical thesis was not only that NBIS is a GPU bare-metal capacity provider. It framed the company as a possible AI-native cloud platform that may move up the stack over time:

AI Infrastructure
↓
AI Cloud Platform
↓
Inference / Token Services
↓
Enterprise AI Tools

What underlying capability does it monetize?

NBIS monetizes access to AI infrastructure today. Higher-value directions may include scheduling, cloud platform services, inference services and token services.

Whether the software layer and platform layer have become material revenue contributors requires verification against current company disclosures.
